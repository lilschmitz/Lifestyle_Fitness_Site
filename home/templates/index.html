  • 2009-2011

    Our Small Beginnings

    Way back in the days we held our bootcamps in Phoenix park and chased our loyal client base around off track utilizing the benches and open space. We have started small and humbly. To date we have remained grateful and indebted to these beginnings.

  • March 2011

    New Equipment

    Our 2011 equipment update has been long anticipated and overdue. With a set of custom fitted Rogue equipment alongside TRX stations we have been able to provide full-body and functional training set up.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Interactive
    Online Service

    In 2012 our services have been made available online utilizing full e-commerce capabilities and functionality. We have been striving to improve our online presence ever since.

  • July 2014

    Phase Two New Location

    Finally, we achieved what we set out to reach - our own gym out in Cherrywood. We will not stop here and we will not rest there. Our location out in the south of Dublin is just another stepping stone to deliver client value!
